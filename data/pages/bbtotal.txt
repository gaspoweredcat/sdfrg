====== Best Practice ======

[[blackberry|Back]]

There are many potential issues with blackberry devices especially because many services for them are being shut down as the OS dies, to get a unit into the best possible state follow these steps:

  - Remove the SIM lock from the device with Chimeratool
  - Change the VendorID to an unbranded version (Such as 301) with Chimeratool
  - (Optional) Change the keyboard layout to match the devices keypad using Chimeratool if needed
  - Full flash the device with a stock firmware via an Autoloader or Chimeratool
  - Reset the device via Blackberry desktop manager

If you follow this flow of processes you should have the unit in the best possible condition for resale
