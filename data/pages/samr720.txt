====== Galaxy Gear S2 R720 ======
\\ 
===== Flashing: =====
\\ 
**NOTE** - This device does not have a USB connection\\ 
\\ 
Flashing is performed via WiFi Odin\\ 
It can also be flashed directly by removing the rear cover and connecting a jig to the test points inside the device\\ 
\\ 
===== Unlocking & FRP: =====
\\ 
Non GSM device, does not require unlocking\\ 
\\ 
FRP/Samsung account cannot currently be removed from this device\\
