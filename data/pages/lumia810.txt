===== Flashing =====
\\ 
  - Open the device folder from the flash files server
  - Run the FullFlash.bat file
  - connect the powered off device to the system and flashing will begin
  - When complete the screen of the device will turn green
  - Disconnect the battery or hold Volume Down and Power to reboot the device
