====== OEM Unlock ======
\\ 
In later versions of android an extra layer of protection was enabled to stop the bootloader being unlocked directly via fastboot, to enable unlocking follow these steps:

  - Boot the device as normal and skip through all startup menus
  - Open Settings
  - Open About phone
  - Tap on the Build Number field 7 times to enable developer mode
  - Return to the settings menu and open the Development Options
  - Enable the OEM Unlock field
  - You can now boot into fastboot or whichever mode is required by your device to unlock the bootloader
