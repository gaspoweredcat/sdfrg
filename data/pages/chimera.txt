====== ChimeraTool ======

===== Information =====

Chimeratool is a multiplatform unlocking tool which focuses mainly on Samsung, Blackberry and LG devices however it is also capable of many operations on other devices such as Huawei, HTC, Generic Chinese units and many more, it is one of the more user friendly tools and generally offers auto detection for many devices.
